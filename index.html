What will you learn in the course?

In the best barista course in history, you will immerse yourself in a world of coffee like no other, where you will learn from the deepest secrets of bean selection to the most advanced preparation techniques. You'll discover the science behind each perfect cup, hone your pouring skills, and develop an authentic connection with coffee and your customers. Get ready to master the art of coffee like you've never imagined before.

Deep Knowledge of Coffee

In the course, you will explore the world of coffee from cultivation to tasting, learning about bean varieties, flavors, growing regions, processing methods, as well as its history and cultural influence on various communities throughout history.

Mastery of Preparation Techniques

The course will teach you all coffee preparation techniques, from espresso to alternative methods such as V60, Chemex or Aeropress. You'll learn to control every variable, like grind and water temperature, to get exceptional results in every cup.

Customer Service Skills Development

In the course, you will learn how to brew exceptional coffee and deliver memorable customer experiences through effective communication skills, understanding individual preferences, and handling challenging situations to maintain a professional and welcoming environment.

Frequent questions

How are classes structured in a fully online course?

In our CafeCraft online course, classes are conducted through an interactive virtual platform. Each session is carefully structured and taught live by our expert instructors. We use a combination of lectures, practical demonstrations, group discussions and hands-on activities to ensure a dynamic and enriching learning experience.

How do you ensure interaction and learning in an online environment?

Despite being an online course, we strive to maintain an interactive and collaborative learning environment. We limit group sizes to a maximum of 10 people to facilitate active participation and personalized attention from instructors. Additionally, we encourage communication through live chat tools, discussion forums, and real-time question and answer sessions.

How do hands-on activities adapt to an online environment?

Although the course is delivered online, we offer a variety of practical activities to ensure a complete learning experience. This may include virtually guided coffee cupping sessions, live video demonstrations of brewing techniques, and hands-on assignments to complete at home. Our instructors are available to provide personalized feedback and additional support throughout the course.
